* Text formattings tags: To display special type of text.


      1. <b> bold : it will display a text in bold.
                    <p>I like <b>blue</b> color</p>


      2. strong: Important text.

                <p>This is <strong>important</strong> text</p>


      3. <i> italic: used to display a text in italic. Technical terms, Phrases, Thoughts etc.
                     <p>it is said that <i>There is no alternate for hard work.</i></p>


      4. <em> emphasized tag : Screen reader will give more stress.
                               <p><em>India</em> is world's top growing country.</p>


      5. <small> : it will display a text in small font.
                   <small>Please enter the username.</small>


      6. <mark>: marked text or highlighted text.
                 <p><mark>Capital of India is Delhi</mark></p>

      7. <del> : it displayes deleted text.
                    <p>I like <del>red</del> blue color</p>


     8. <ins> :  it is used to display inserted text.
                 <p>Capital city of Maharashtra is <ins>Mumbai</ins>.</p>



     9. <sup> :  to display superscripted text.    Mathematical, checmical formulas etc.
                 <p>a<sup>2</sup>b</p>

     10. <sub> : subscript , to display subscripted text.
                 <p>H<sub>2</sub>O</p>
